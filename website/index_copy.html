What i do ? I am computational social scientist with management experience focused on applied work, including program evaluation and intervention design. My background is in computer science, experimental social psychology, economics, sociology and statistics. Though I focus on quantitative work, my work often involves mixed methods. Topically, I specialize in work related to education, income, health and racial/ethnic differences. I am also interested in intersections with social good, public policy and technology, as well as academic work.
